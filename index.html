Privacy Policy for BondhuGo

Last Updated: November 12, 2025

Thank you for choosing BondhuGo ("we," "us," "our"). This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

By using BondhuGo, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

To provide and improve our service, we collect the following types of information:

a. Information You Provide to Us

  • Profile Information: When you create or update your profile, you provide us with your name, a unique public username (@handle), date of birth, and gender. You may also choose to add a bio.
  • User Content: We collect the content you create on our platform, including posts and comments.
  • Saved Content: We store a list of the posts you save so that you can view them later.

b. Information Collected Automatically

  • Google Sign-In: When you sign up using Google, we receive personal information from your Google account, such as your name, email address, and profile picture. We only use this for authentication and to populate your initial profile.
  • Search History: To make it easier to find people again, your recent search history is stored locally on your device. This information is private to you and is not sent to our servers.
  • Usage Data: We use Firebase services, which may automatically collect data about how you interact with our app, such as device information and crash reports, to help us improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and Maintain the Service: To create and manage your account, display your profile with your username, and show your posts to others.
  • Personalize Your Experience: To provide features like Saved Posts and Search History.
  • Ensure Security: To protect your account and our platform from fraud and abuse.
  • Improve Our App: To understand how our users interact with BondhuGo so we can fix bugs and build new features.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Storage: Your data, including your profile information, username, and posts, is securely stored on Google Cloud Firestore. Your search history is stored only on your local device.
  • Security: We take the security of your data seriously. We use Firestore Security Rules to protect your data from unauthorized access. Only you can edit your own profile and delete your own content.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on the following third-party services, which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Sign-In: Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase & Google Cloud Firestore: Firebase Privacy Policy

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access and control your personal data.

  • Access and Update: You can view and edit your profile information (name, username, and bio) at any time through the app settings.
  • Data Control: You can delete your own posts and comments. You can also view and clear your on-device search history from within the app. To delete your entire account, please contact us.

6. Children's Privacy

BondhuGo is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy within the app or on our website. You are advised to review this policy periodically.
